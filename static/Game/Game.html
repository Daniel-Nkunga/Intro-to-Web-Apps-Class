Filler!

Filler is a Game Pidgeon fame for IOS where the goal is to get the most colors as possible. Read more about the rules at the bottom of the page

How to play Filler

- To win, get the majority of the board under your control
- You can either choose a tile on the board or select the from the buttons at the bottom of the board to claim a color
- You CANNOT chose a color that is already chosen by a player

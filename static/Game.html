Filler!

Filler is a Game Pidgeon game for iOS where the goal is to get the most colors as possible. Read more about the rules at the bottom of the page

Choose a Color!

How to play Filler

- To win, get the majority of the board under your control
- You can either choose a tile on the board or select from the buttons at the bottom of the board to claim a color
- You CANNOT choose a color that is already chosen by a player
